Information for Residents & Families

The EnRICH team provides specialist geriatrician care to residents in care homes across Cambridgeshire. Here is what you need to know about our service.

What We Do

EnRICH (Enhanced Reviews in Care Homes) brings specialist geriatrician expertise directly into care homes. Our team works alongside care home staff and GPs to provide:

  • Comprehensive health assessments — thorough reviews of physical, mental, and social wellbeing
  • Medication reviews — ensuring medications are appropriate and optimised
  • Advance care planning — respectful conversations about future wishes and preferences
  • Rapid response — specialist support when residents become unwell, helping avoid unnecessary hospital trips where safe to do so
  • Continuity of care — the same team provides care in the community and hospital, ensuring no information is lost

💬 How to Access the EnRICH Service

Although families and residents cannot refer to us directly, you can request a review through your care home. Here's how:

1 Speak to a member of care home staff about your concerns or the concerns you have about your loved one.

2 Discuss whether an EnRICH referral would be appropriate. Care home staff can discuss with the GP if needed.

3 A care home staff member or GP will refer to the EnRICH team on your behalf using their secure EnRICH PIN code.

What to Expect

If a referral is made, a member of the EnRICH team will visit the care home to carry out an assessment. This will typically include:

  • A conversation with the resident (and family members if they wish to be involved)
  • A review of the resident's medical history and current health
  • Discussion with care home staff about day-to-day care needs
  • A personalised care plan developed in partnership with the resident, family, and care team

We believe in person-centred care and will always seek to understand and respect the wishes and preferences of each individual resident.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about the EnRICH service, you can ask a member of care home staff to put you in touch with our team. For general enquiries, the care home team can email cuh.carehomesteam@nhs.net.

For emergency situations, always call 999.
