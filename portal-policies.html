Clinical Protocols

Key clinical protocols and care pathways used by the EnRICH service.

  • Acute Deterioration Protocol — When and how to escalate concerns about a resident's condition
  • Falls Assessment Pathway — Post-fall assessment checklist and onward referral criteria
  • Delirium Screening Protocol — 4AT screening tool guidance and management pathway
  • End of Life Care Pathway — Symptom management and comfort care guidelines

Medication Management

  • Polypharmacy Review Guidance — STOPPFrail criteria and deprescribing framework
  • Anticipatory Medications — Standard anticipatory prescribing for end of life care
  • Antibiotic Guidelines — Community-onset infection management in care homes
  • Controlled Drug Procedures — Safe storage, administration, and record-keeping

Escalation & Communication

  • SBAR Communication Tool — Structured handover format for clinical escalation
  • When to Call 999 — Clear criteria for emergency ambulance activation
  • EnRICH Referral Criteria — Which residents benefit from EnRICH input
  • Hospital Transfer Checklist — Essential documentation and information to send with residents
